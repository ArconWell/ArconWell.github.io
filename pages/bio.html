MY STORY
Hello! My name is Cody. Cody Hanson. I was born and raised in North Carolina. My father is a farmer and my mother is a music teacher at school. It was she who instilled in me a love of music and taught me to play the guitar. I started writing my first songs as a child, and started performing at various concerts already in high school. After high school, I decided to enter The Juilliard School, New York. I was accepted for the "Bachelor of Music". I studied and worked as a musician in a small restaurant on the outskirts of the city. There I also met my best friend, who helped me in my career. At first I recorded tracks as part of a group, but after that I decided to go solo in the world of music. In my compositions, I tried to reflect the life of ordinary people and the love for nature that I have carried with me since childhood.
